My Childhood Swag

As a child growing up, I loved dressing up for the occasion - mainly for my religious holiday. Being that I didn't know much about fashion, my mom would ask me what color I'd prefer, and she'd go ahead and find what she believe is the best in that color. Oh, and I found my shades obsession that lived on forever.

I was always a very shy and introverted child, so my clothes were my primary way to express myself. I'd dress for Eid (an Islamic holiday), my birthday, Halloween and more.
